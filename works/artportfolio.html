Art Portfolio

  1. Full Responsive Website
  2. Front and Backend Design
  3. Coded from Scratch

A website built to showcase my paintings, drawings, and other art projects. Designed and coded from scratch, using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP. The aim was to create a space to view all of my art in a clean, easy to use environment. With the addition of an interactive introduction page, I aimed to stray a bit from common everyday templated site to integrate creative arts in the design of the site itself. The site also acts as a space to contact me, and inquire about a specific piece, although not the site's main function, thus left out of the forefront of the design.

Frekka

  1. Full site build and design with cargo
  2. Custom Html and CSS

Frekka.com is a portfolio site for designer Francesca Heidig, I designed and built the site with cargo in conjunction with adding custom HTML and CSS. Cargo was chosen to allow the client ease of uploading new images and pages independently after the site was created. The main focus of the site is to highlight her work and process while limiting excess clutter.

SCOPE - Design Associate

  1. Print
  2. Digital

SCOPE is an International Contemporary Art show, focusing on showcasing premier art galleries in the contemporary art space. SCOPE has garnered extensive critical acclaim with cumulative sales well over one billion dollars and attendance of 1.2 million visitors. Their last Miami beach show greeted over 70,000 visitors. I worked as a design associate, alongside the Head of design and Director of SCOPE to create all print and digital deign materials for the 2019 Miami Beach and 2020 New York shows. The work consisted of but was not limited to, ad campaigns, onsite print material design, signage throughout the show and web development. In addition, during weeks prior to the shows I worked onsite with out of house crews to oversee the quality and installation of onsite signage.
